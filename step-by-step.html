Step by Step Guide

Start with Education

Currently studying New Media Communication at UniMAP — this is where I’m building my foundation in creativity, storytelling, and digital thinking.

Build a Portfolio

Every class project, side hustle, and idea counts. I’m slowly shaping a portfolio that reflects not just my skills, but also my voice and style.

Gain Experience

From internships to freelance gigs, I’m actively looking for chances to get my hands dirty in the real creative world — because that’s where the magic happens.

Keep Learning, Always

I take online courses in branding, design thinking, UX/UI, and leadership — I try to always crave for a new knowledge. Staying sharp is part of the process.

Stay Inspired, Stay Current

I follow creative directors, designers, and storytellers who inspire me — always learning from the best, always watching what’s next.

Collaborate & Volunteer

I join community projects and help out where I can — not just for the experience, but to connect, to grow, and to give back.

Master Soft Skills

Creative direction isn’t just about visuals. I’m working on my communication, leadership, and presentation skills so I can lead with confidence and empathy.

Join a Creative Team

The goal? Land a role in a creative department — somewhere I can contribute, learn from the pros, and grow into the role I dream of.

Grow with Feedback

Seek mentorship and continuously refine your work through constructive feedback.

Pitch Your Ideas

Take initiative, propose creative concepts, and lead projects with confidence.

Step Into The Role

Someday, I’ll step fully into the role of Creative Director — but until then, I’ll keep creating, learning, and leading in my own way. Legacy takes time, and I’m in no rush to do it right.

Each step is a milestone. Keep moving forward with passion and purpose!
