A Work-in-Progress (and Proud)

I’m still a student, just getting started — but trust me, I’ve got big dreams. Creative direction isn’t just a title to me; it’s the kind of work I want to grow into. Right now, I’m learning through classes, late-night projects, failed ideas that turned into better ones, and just... showing up, again and again. This site isn’t about having it all figured out — it’s about building something while figuring it out. Kind of like a creative journal, just a little more dressed up. Born to Create: My Aspiration to Become a Creative Director

My dream is to become a Creative Director — a visionary leader who brings ideas to life through storytelling, design, and strategic thinking. I am inspired by how Creative Directors guide the creative process from concept to execution, ensuring every visual, message, and experience aligns with a brand’s identity. They are not only designers but also mentors, communicators, and problem-solvers. I aspire to one day lead creative teams, shape compelling brand narratives, and use the power of design to connect with people, spark emotion, and drive innovation. It's more than a title — it's a role that combines artistry with leadership to make a real impact.

VISION & INSPIRATION

I’m inspired by the little details in everyday life and how they can be turned into meaningful visuals. My vision is to craft creative work that speaks honestly, feels personal, and connects with people—whether it's through editing, storytelling, or visual content.

SKILLS I'M BUILDING

I’m currently sharpening my skills in editing, visual communication, creative writing, and digital storytelling—especially with tools like Adobe Suite and AI like ChatGPT. I’m also learning how to plan content with intention and emotion.

MILESTONE & GROWTH

From uni projects to personal experiments, each step is helping me grow into a more confident and thoughtful creator. My biggest growth? Learning to trust my ideas, explore new styles, and express myself without overthinking.
